The Hut.

The Shepherd's Hut has been completely renovated and dates back to around 1910 when it was used by Shepherds during the lambing season on the Dorset Downs. It has been lovingly restored and is now fully insulated and comfortably furnished.

In the evenings the hut is heated by a wood burner and lit by solar powered lighting. When you wake, you will find a breakfast hamper full of local produce has been delivered to your door for you to enjoy at your leisure.

The Area.

Though only a little more than a mile away from the town of Shaftesbury, our serene and secluded location in Melbury Abbas offers you the perfect respite from the cares and stresses of the modern world without having to jump on a plane.

  • Less than one mile to the A30 by car.
  • Only 2 hours from London by train.
  • Directly on the national cycle highway.
  • At the end of the ancient Wessex ridgeway footpath.
  • Less than an hour to the golden beaches of Studland Bay.
